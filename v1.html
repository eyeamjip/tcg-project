Frequently asked

Quick answers.

How much does cleaning cost in Las Vegas?

Pricing depends on the service, square footage, and condition of the space. Window cleaning typically starts around $150 for a small home. Move-out and post-construction jobs are quoted by walkthrough or photo. Send us a quote request and we'll give you a real number, not a placeholder.

Are you licensed and insured?

Yes — fully licensed in Nevada, bonded, and carrying liability insurance. We can send proof of insurance for any commercial job that requires it.

Do you do recurring service?

We do. Most of our commercial clients are weekly, bi-weekly, or monthly. Window cleaning is most often quarterly or twice a year for homes. Set the cadence that fits and we lock in a recurring slot.

Ready to get on the schedule?

Send us the details and we'll get back to you the same day, often within an hour during business hours.
