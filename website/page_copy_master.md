### Intro paragraph
A finished build is not a clean build. Drywall dust gets into vents, sticker residue stays on every appliance, and silica settles on every surface in the unit. We handle the final clean so your client walks into a finished space — not a finished mess.

### Who we work with
- General contractors finishing residential builds and renovations
- Commercial builders turning over retail, office, and restaurant buildouts
- Homeowners completing renovations who don't want to clean up after the crew
- Property developers prepping new units for buyers or tenants

### Our three-phase approach

Construction cleaning is rarely one pass. We work in phases so the right level of cleaning happens at the right point in your project.

**1. Rough clean**
After framing and rough trades. Large debris removal, broom-and-haul on construction waste, surface dust knockdown so finishers can work in a cleaner environment.

**2. Final clean**
After all trades are out. The detailed pass — every surface, every fixture. Sticker and adhesive removal from windows and appliances, cabinet interiors, light fixtures, vents, baseboards, hardware polished, floors deep-cleaned.

**3. Touch-up clean**
Right before walkthrough or handover. Final dust pass, fingerprint and footprint cleanup, glass redone, anything the day-of inspection would catch.

You can book one phase or all three. Most contractors book the final clean and the touch-up as a package.

### What's specifically included in a final clean
- All windows, interior and exterior — sticker and label removal
- All glass surfaces and mirrors
- Cabinet interiors and exteriors, hardware polished
- Appliances inside and out, all factory packaging removed
- Light fixtures and ceiling fans
- HVAC vents and registers (surface only — duct cleaning is a separate trade)
- Outlets and switch plates wiped
- Baseboards, door frames, and door interiors
- Tile and grout cleaned, hard floors mopped, carpets vacuumed (or steam quoted separately)
- Final debris haul

### Why contractors keep our number

We show up on the day we say we'll be there. We carry our own insurance with limits that meet most general contractor requirements (proof on request). We can phase around your finishing trades. And we do not leave a punch list of "we'll come back tomorrow" — when we leave, the job is done.

Post-construction is typically priced by square footage. In the Las Vegas market, residential post-construction runs roughly $0.20 to $0.40 per square foot for a final clean, with rough cleans on the lower end and detailed condo or restaurant builds on the higher end. We quote every job in writing after a walkthrough or photo set.
