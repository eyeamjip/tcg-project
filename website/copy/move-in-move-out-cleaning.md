## What we clean on a move-out job

### Kitchen
Inside the oven, inside the fridge, inside cabinets and drawers, behind the stove if it pulls out, microwave, dishwasher exterior, sinks and faucets, countertops, backsplash, all surfaces wiped down.

### Bathrooms
Toilets, tubs, showers, glass doors, tile and grout, vanities inside and out, mirrors, exhaust fans, baseboards.

### Bedrooms and living areas
Closets and shelves, ceiling fans, light fixtures, windowsills, baseboards, switch plates, doors and door frames, floors fully cleaned (vacuum + mop or steam).

### The details landlords check
Blinds, vents, behind doors, the inside of the front-load washer and dryer if accessible, and the back of the toilet — the spots that make or break a deposit inspection.

## How long does it take?

A small one-bedroom apartment is usually a half-day. A 2,000+ square foot home runs a full day with a crew of two. We give you a window when we quote, not a vague estimate.

Move cleanings are quoted by square footage and condition. As a reference: a one-bedroom apartment typically runs $250 to $400. A 3-bedroom home runs $400 to $700. Heavy condition adds to the quote — we tell you before we start, never after.

## FAQ

### How much does move out cleaning cost in Las Vegas?
Most one-bedroom apartments run $250–$400. Three-bedroom homes typically run $400–$700. Larger or heavily soiled spaces are quoted on walkthrough. Get a written quote in advance so there are no surprises.

### Will your cleaning meet my landlord's checklist?
Yes. Send us your landlord's or property manager's move-out checklist when you book and we'll work straight from it. If anything is missed when they inspect, we come back and fix it free.

### Can I book move out cleaning the day I'm moving?
We recommend booking 3–7 days out, but we keep some same-week capacity open for last-minute moves. Call or text 702-551-4878 if you need us this week.

### Do you bring your own supplies?
Yes — all equipment, vacuums, and cleaning products. You don't need to leave anything behind.

### Do you clean inside the oven and fridge?
Yes, both are included in every move cleaning by default. Some companies charge extra for these — we don't.
