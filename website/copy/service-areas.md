## Cities and neighborhoods we serve

### Las Vegas
We cover all of Las Vegas proper, including:
- **Downtown Las Vegas** — historic core, Fremont Street area
- **The Strip** — hospitality, retail, and offices on Las Vegas Boulevard
- **Summerlin** — master-planned community on the west side
- **Centennial Hills** — far northwest, near US-95

### Henderson
Henderson is one of our top service areas. We serve homes, offices, and retail across Green Valley, Anthem, MacDonald Highlands, and the rest of Henderson.

### North Las Vegas
Residential and commercial cleaning across North Las Vegas, including the Aliante area and central NLV.

### Spring Valley
Spring Valley homes and businesses, especially near the 215 corridor and west of the Strip.

### Enterprise
Growing community south of the Strip — single-family homes, new builds, and emerging retail.

### Paradise
Hotels, offices, and homes in the Paradise area, including the airport corridor.

### Sunrise Manor
East-side residential and commercial coverage.

### Green Valley
Established neighborhoods of Henderson — homes, offices, and retail. One of our most consistent recurring service areas.

### Southern Highlands
Master-planned community in the south valley with new builds, larger homes, and a growing commercial corridor — ideal for both window and post-construction cleaning.

### Rhodes Ranch
Master-planned community in the southwest valley. Recurring residential window cleaning is popular here.

### Whitney
East valley residential and small commercial.
