## How often should you clean your windows?

For most Las Vegas homes, twice a year is the sweet spot — once in spring after the wind season, once in fall after summer dust settles. Homes near new construction or in dustier zones (Centennial Hills, the outer ring of Summerlin, Southern Highlands) often need quarterly service. Storefronts and restaurants almost always need monthly or bi-weekly cleaning to keep the glass looking owned.

## FAQ (with FAQ schema)

### How much does window cleaning cost in Las Vegas?
Most residential window cleanings in Las Vegas range from $150 to $400 depending on the number of windows, the home's height, and whether you want screens and tracks included. Commercial storefronts are typically priced per visit on a recurring contract. We give every quote in writing so there are no surprises.

### Do you clean second-story exterior windows?
Yes. We use water-fed poles and extension ladders for two-story homes. For homes taller than two stories, we send a tech to evaluate before quoting.

### Will my windows streak in the Las Vegas heat?
This is the most common worry, and it is a fair one — water dries fast here. We use professional squeegees, fresh distilled-water mixes, and we work in the right order (top down, dry as we go). If you ever see a streak after we leave, call us back and we fix it.

### Do you clean the screens too?
Yes. We remove the screens, rinse them down, and reinstall them as part of every standard window cleaning. If a screen is damaged we tell you before doing anything to it.

### Do you offer recurring window cleaning?
Yes — monthly, bi-monthly, quarterly, or twice a year. Recurring clients get priority scheduling and a small repeat-customer discount.
