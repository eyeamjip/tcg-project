## Window cleaning

### How much does window cleaning cost in Las Vegas?
Most residential window cleanings in Las Vegas range from $150 to $400 depending on the number of windows, the home's height, and whether you want screens and tracks included. Commercial storefronts are typically priced per visit on a recurring contract.

### Do you clean second-story exterior windows?
Yes. We use water-fed poles and extension ladders for two-story homes. For homes taller than two stories, we send a tech to evaluate before quoting.

### Will my windows streak in the Las Vegas heat?
Water dries fast here, which is why technique matters. We use professional squeegees, fresh distilled-water mixes, and we work top down, drying as we go. If you ever see a streak after we leave, call us back and we fix it.

### Do you clean the screens too?
Yes. We remove the screens, rinse them down, and reinstall them as part of every standard window cleaning. If a screen is damaged we tell you before doing anything to it.

### How often should I get my windows cleaned?
For most Las Vegas homes, twice a year is the sweet spot — once in spring, once in fall. Homes near construction or in dustier zones often need quarterly service. Storefronts and restaurants almost always need monthly or bi-weekly cleaning.

## Move in / move out cleaning

### How much does move out cleaning cost in Las Vegas?
Most one-bedroom apartments run $250–$400. Three-bedroom homes typically run $400–$700. Larger or heavily soiled spaces are quoted on walkthrough.

### Will your cleaning meet my landlord's checklist?
Yes. Send us your landlord's or property manager's move-out checklist when you book and we'll work straight from it. If anything is missed when they inspect, we come back and fix it free.

### Can I book the day I'm moving?
We recommend booking 3–7 days out, but we keep some same-week capacity open for last-minute moves. Call or text 702-551-4878 if you need us this week.

### Do you bring your own supplies?
Yes — all equipment, vacuums, and cleaning products. You don't need to leave anything behind.

### Do you clean inside the oven and fridge?
Yes, both are included in every move cleaning by default. Some companies charge extra for these — we don't.

## Post construction cleaning

### How much does post construction cleaning cost in Las Vegas?
Most post-construction cleaning in Las Vegas prices between $0.20 and $0.40 per square foot for a final clean. A 2,500 sq ft new build typically runs $500–$1,000 depending on condition.

### Do you clean during construction or only at the end?
Both. We phase: rough clean during construction, final clean before handover, touch-up clean right before walkthrough. Book one or all three.

### Are you insured for commercial construction sites?
Yes — fully licensed, bonded, and insured. We carry general liability and can provide a certificate of insurance to general contractors and property owners on request.

### How fast can you turn around a final clean?
For most homes and units, we can complete a final clean in one day with a two- or three-person crew. Larger commercial buildouts take 2–3 days.

### Do you remove construction debris and haul it away?
We handle surface debris and bag it for haul. Large construction waste — pallets, drywall sheets, lumber — is a contractor or dumpster trade. We can recommend local haulers if you need one.

## Commercial cleaning

### How much does commercial cleaning cost in Las Vegas?
Most small office and retail spaces in Las Vegas run $150–$350 per recurring visit, depending on square footage and frequency. Restaurants and larger spaces are quoted on walkthrough.

### Can you clean after business hours?
Yes. Most of our office and retail clients are cleaned after close or before open. We have a key-and-alarm protocol and can work around your schedule.

### Do I have to sign a long-term contract?
No. Our commercial agreements are month-to-month. You can pause or cancel with 30 days' notice — no auto-renewals, no cancellation fees.

### Do you handle restaurant kitchen cleaning?
We clean dining rooms, restrooms, glass, and front-of-house. Kitchen hood and exhaust cleaning is a specialized trade — we partner with local hood-cleaning specialists and can refer you.
