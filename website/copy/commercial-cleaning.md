## Who we clean for

### Offices
Daily, nightly, weekly, or bi-weekly service. Trash, restrooms, kitchens, conference rooms, common areas, glass partitions. We can work after hours so your team never sees us.

### Retail
Sales floor, fitting rooms, restrooms, glass, and storefront windows. Most retail clients book us before opening, before close, or both.

### Restaurants
Front of house deep cleans, restrooms, dining areas, glass, entryways. Back-of-house and kitchen hood cleaning is a separate trade — happy to recommend partners.

### Salons and spas
Stations, restrooms, floors, mirrors, glass, common areas. Hair-heavy floors are part of the job — we have the right tools for it.

## Our recurring service model

Most commercial clients land on one of three cadences:

### Daily
For high-traffic offices, restaurants, and retail. Restrooms, trash, surfaces, floors. Quick, dependable, never an excuse.

### Weekly or bi-weekly
For mid-traffic offices, salons, and small retail. Full deep clean each visit including glass, baseboards, and vents on rotation.

### Monthly + as-needed deep cleans
For low-traffic spaces or businesses that handle daily tidying in-house and need us for the deeper work.

## FAQ

### How much does commercial cleaning cost in Las Vegas?
Most small office and retail spaces in Las Vegas run $150–$350 per recurring visit, depending on square footage and frequency. Restaurants and larger spaces are quoted on walkthrough. Recurring clients always get a better rate than one-time service.

### Can you clean after business hours?
Yes. Most of our office and retail clients are cleaned after close or before open. We have a key-and-alarm protocol and can work around your schedule.

### Do I have to sign a long-term contract?
No. Our commercial agreements are month-to-month. You can pause or cancel with 30 days' notice — no auto-renewals, no cancellation fees.

### Are your cleaners background-checked?
Yes. Every team member is background-checked and trained on our process. We carry full liability insurance and a janitorial bond.

### Do you handle restaurant kitchen cleaning?
We clean dining rooms, restrooms, glass, and front-of-house. Kitchen hood and exhaust cleaning is a specialized trade — we partner with local hood-cleaning specialists and can refer you.
