## FAQ (with FAQ schema)

### How much does house cleaning cost in Las Vegas?
Most recurring residential cleanings in Las Vegas range from $140 (1-bed apartment) to $300+ (3–4 bedroom homes). One-time deep cleans start around $185 for small spaces and scale up by square footage and condition. We quote every job in writing — no surprises after we show up.

### Do you bring your own supplies?
Yes. We bring everything — vacuum, mop, microfiber cloths, eco-friendly all-purpose products. If you prefer we use your products (allergies, brand preferences, scent sensitivities), just tell us and we will.

### Is the same person cleaning every time?
For recurring clients, yes — we assign a primary team and they cover your visits. Continuity matters: same team means we know the quirks of your home and you don't have to re-explain anything. If your regular tech is sick or on PTO, we send a covering tech with notes.

### What about my pets?
We're pet-friendly — most of our homes have at least one. Just tell us about your pets in the intake form (names, temperament, any "don't open this door" notes) and we'll work around them. If your dog is the kind who needs the cleaning lady to be a friend first, we can do a meet-and-greet visit before the first clean.

### Do I need to be home during the cleaning?
No. Most of our recurring clients give us a code, hide-a-key, or front-door smart lock access. You decide. We text when we arrive and when we leave.

### What if I need to skip or reschedule a visit?
Just text us 24 hours in advance and we'll move it. No fee, no judgment. Last-minute cancels (within 24 hours) we charge a $50 visit-prep fee since we already routed the day.

### Do you do laundry?
Yes — up to 2 loads (wash/dry/fold) as an add-on. We don't iron or dry-clean.

### Will you clean inside my oven and fridge?
Yes — both are add-ons (not standard) because they take significant extra time. Add them when you book or anytime in advance of a visit.

### What's the difference between a regular clean and a deep clean?
Regular cleaning maintains a home that's already clean — surfaces, floors, bathrooms, kitchen. A deep clean is for homes that haven't been professionally cleaned in 3+ months: detailed work on baseboards, inside appliances, grout, tile, vents, blinds, etc. We recommend a one-time deep clean as the first visit for new recurring clients.

### Are you licensed and insured?
Yes — fully licensed in Nevada, bonded, and carrying liability insurance. We can send proof of insurance for any HOA or property manager that requires it.

### Do you have a satisfaction guarantee?
Yes. If something's missed, text us within 48 hours and we'll come back to fix it — free.
